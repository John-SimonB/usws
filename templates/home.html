Project Description

Facing todays problems

Inflation is being felt in every area of life. Especially students or people with low incomes are hit hard. That's why we want to remedy the situation in the most essential area of life: FOOD! With our prototype in Unternehmenssoftware in SS 2022, people can view affordable recipes and also order directly from Flink. Meal planning and keeping a better overview of the budget has never been so easy! On our dashboard, you can also view the different price trends of the products on Flink and also see other nice-to-know visualizations. The visualizations are about the price and industry developments in Germany regarding food and what german eating habits are like.
